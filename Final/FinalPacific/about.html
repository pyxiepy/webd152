The Way of the Trail

Take a deep draught of the healing elixer of elements where they converge deep in the beating heart of the Pacific Trails. Daily monotony of domicile walls gives way to the expansive, eternal grandeur of the great outdoors when you extend your will and awareness... taking your first step unto the path that recedes into the sapphire horizon - the gritty trail that ascends over the ridge into the unknown...

Open to the riches and generosity of the Pacific Trails... to the plethora of treasures they have to bestow upon your personal and our collective history.

It's the simple things... the simple mysteries that reveal their truth to you and to the you-niverse only when you venture out of your comfort zone, and into the vast comforts, the satisfying challenges, and fulfilling abundance of the natural realm.
